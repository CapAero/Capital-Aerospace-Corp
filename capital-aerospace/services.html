Utility Helicopter Maintenance & Field Support

At Capital Aerospace Corp, we specialize in delivering comprehensive helicopter maintenance services designed for the unique demands of utility aviation. From routine inspections to complex rebuilds, our skilled technicians ensure your aircraft operates at peak performance—wherever your mission takes you.

Our expertise covers the full spectrum of maintenance, including scheduled inspections, AOG troubleshooting, custom equipment installations, major 12-year and annual inspections, complete paint refinishing, and bespoke rebuilds tailored to owner and operator requirements.

We pride ourselves on our field operations capability—deploying maintenance teams directly to your location to minimize downtime and keep your operations moving. Whether in remote northern regions or high-demand project environments, our goal is simple: maximum aircraft availability with uncompromising safety and quality.

Every project is driven by our commitment to pilot and client satisfaction. We understand that your helicopter is more than just equipment—it’s the lifeline of your operation. That’s why we work closely with crews and operators to deliver timely, reliable, and mission-ready results that keep you flying with confidence.
